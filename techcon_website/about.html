Our History

TechCon started in 2010 as a small meetup of 50 passionate developers in San Francisco. Since then, it has grown into one of the world's leading technology conferences, attracting over 10,000 attendees annually from more than 80 countries.

Our Mission

To inspire innovation, foster collaboration, and accelerate the future of technology by bringing together the brightest minds in software development, AI, cloud computing, and digital transformation.

We believe in inclusive, accessible, and forward-thinking tech education for all.
